HIGHER GAME

532 days. 40,000 data points. One woman who refused to lose.

Hosted by Stephanie Belote

I lost my mind at a game of Candyland when I was four. Now at 54, I'm playing for higher stakes.

Higher Game is the podcast for people building something from nothing using AI, grit, and the refusal to stay on someone else's board. A 54-year-old woman with no tech background who taught herself to code inside The Real World, the world's largest online university platform—and built Cortex, her own agentic AI memory system that finds patterns across years of data no human brain could hold at once.

This is an obsessive look at systems building from the inside out. From pattern recognition to AI automation, it's a show for the obsessive learners, the late-blooming tech entrepreneurs, and the women over 50 rewriting the rules.

The most dangerous person in any room is the one nobody saw coming.
